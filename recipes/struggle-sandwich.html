Struggle Sandwich

Description

It's ice between two pieces of bread. Enjoy the struggle.

Ingredients

  • Bread, the cheaper the better
  • Ice
  • Plate (OPTIONAL)

Steps

  1. Lay two pieces of bread on flat surface
  2. Place ice on one piece of bread
  3. Cover ice with other piece of bread
  4. There is no enjoyment in this. Most likely you ruined two good pieces of bread
